About Us

Anemon is a metal band inspired by the power that emerges from the depths of the sea. With dark melodies and powerful rhythms, they take listeners on a mystical journey.

"It's evolution through distortion" - This philosophy forms the foundation of our band. Sound distortion is not just an effect, but the very essence of musical evolution.

Our band creates a unique musical experience by blending nature's most primitive forces with modern metal sounds.
